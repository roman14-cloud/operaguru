about us

We offer courier services across the country including office collections where clients collect parcels from our offices in their region, and last mile (door-to-door) deliveries. We handle deliveries for prepaid orders as well as cash-on-delivery orders. All orders are tracked by our customer service team in liaison with the courier client.

Fast delivery We deliver parcels within 24 hours from dispatch time. secured service Do not open policy, Integrity, Cash on delivery parcels confirmation, insurance cover to facilitate quick compensation contrywide shipping We do deliveries across the country (Kenya). We have major offices in Nairobi (Headquarters along Mombasa Road), Nairobi CBD, Mombasa, Nakuru, Eldoret, Kisumu, Kisii, Voi, Meru, Kakamega and Machakos.
